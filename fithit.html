FitHIT

Build any workout!

FitHIT is a sleek and easy to use interval timer and workout logger for athletes in training. It is perfect for CrossFit, HIIT, tabata and more. Just create your workout and press Start, or choose from our built in workouts.

Features

Focus on your workout

Almost no navigation required! FitHIT lets you concentrate on your workout. Either switch automatically to the next exercise, or double tap anywhere on the screen to start the next one. Clear visual cues keep you updated on progress and lets you know what's up next.

5 Build in programms

FitHIT comes with 5 built-in workouts: Fight Gone Bad, Cindy, Filthy 50, Fran and Helen.

Designed
for gym

FitHIT's workout screen uses large fonts to display current exercise information and assigns different screen colours to each exercise, so you know when it's time to change exercise without interrupting your workout.
