FitHIT

Personalize your workout!

FitHIT is a sleek and easy to use interval timer and workout logger for athletes in training. Perfect for CrossFit, HIIT, tabata & more.

Features

Focus on your workout

Simpliest navigation ever! FitHIT lets you concentrate on your workout. Set it to automatically switch to the next exercise, or double tap anywhere on the screen to start the next one. Clear visual cues keep you updated on progress and let you know what's up next.

5 built-in programs

FitHIT comes with 5 built-in & ready-to-use workouts: Fight Gone Bad, Cindy, Filthy 50, Fran & Helen. Don't scratch your head over what to do next, just select one of the existing programs!

Designed for use
in the gym

FitHIT's workout screen uses large fonts to display current exercise information and assigns different screen colours to each exercise, so you know when it's time to change exercise without interrupting your workout. Want something similar?
