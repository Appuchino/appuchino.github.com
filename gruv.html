Grüv

e-scooter cruising in California

Grüv is a US-based e-scooter rental service. Appuchino team picked up the app development in the middle of the project and managed to save the day for the customer by delivering the final app release in an extremely tight timeframe.

Platforms: iOS and Android


Achievements

How do we know we did a good job?

Successfully released the app in Oakland and San Jose within a super tight timeframe.

Took effort and responsibility to improve the old version of the app initially released in Chicago.

App users made over 130,000 rides and traveled over 280,000 km. That's seven times around the equator!

Also shipped a backstage version of the app used by eScooter maintenance operators.

    Features

    Locating a scooter

    The app allows users to locate the nearest scooters in the area available for booking and use the map to navigate to the location. Special zones, e.g., parking or restricted zones, can be highlighted on the map.

    Managing personal wallet

    The riders could pay for their rides by topping up the wallet or purchasing a monthly subscription. The app supports Paypal, Mastercard, and other major payment system providers.

    Features

    Ride History

    Users can see detailed information about all of their previous rides. That includes distance, time as well as the total amount paid for each ride.

    Operator Mode

    A separate mode for maintenance teams displays all vehicles' status (battery charge, availability, maintenance need) and allows them to control scooters for transportation. Operator mode has filters to show only vehicles with a certain status.
