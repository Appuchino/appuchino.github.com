Darto

Live train info for Dublin commuters

Our goal was to create an easy to use and fresh looking app for Dublin rail commuters.
We also developed some key features unique to the market, like train alerts and arrivals widget.

Features

Smart alerts

Darto allows you to set an alarm for a specific train. It will alert you when it's time to leave home to catch your ride.

Today Widget

Train times for your favourite and nearest stations just a swipe away. You don't even need to open the app!

Turn your idea into working app

Our target was to create nice looking and very easy to use daily commute app. Users really like Darto.
At the moment it has 135 reviews on the Irish App Store with average rating 4.6 stars!
