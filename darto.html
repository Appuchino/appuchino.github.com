Darto

Train schedule for Dublin commuters

Darto is a smart, simple & beatiful app for Dublin train commuters. Never miss your train again!

Features

Smart alerts

Darto allows you to set an alarm for a specific train. It will alert you when it's time to leave home to catch your ride.

Location-based

Darto will automatically display train schedule for the DART station closest to your location.
